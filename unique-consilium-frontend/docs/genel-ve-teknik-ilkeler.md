# UNIQUE CONSILIUM — General & Technical Principles (2025)

## General
- Perfectionism; zero tolerance for mediocrity
- Premium corporate identity, clean/minimal
- Ultra speed, stability, accessibility (“2-click rule”)
- Single language (English)
- Social proof & fixed page set
- GDPR/KVKK, maintainability, cost awareness
- Step-by-step QA with approvals

## Tech
Frontend: Next.js (App Router, TS), @carbon/react, Carbon SCSS/Sass, Redux Toolkit  
Backend: Django + DRF, PostgreSQL, Redis, Celery, Nginx, Docker, CI/CD, WAF, 2FA

## Design

- Carbon Grid & tokens (no Tailwind)
- SEO: OG/meta/schema, sitemap.xml, robots.txt, manifest.json
